name: Pay-per-call conversion focus
description: The only goal of this site is getting visitors to tap the call button — all design and content decisions must serve that
type: feedback
---

The site is a pay-per-call funnel. The sole conversion goal is getting people to call. Do not add forms, chat widgets, or alternate conversion paths.

**Why:** Every element should reduce hesitation and push toward the one action: dialing the phone number.

**How to apply:** When suggesting or building features, always ask "does this make someone more likely to call right now?" If not, don't add it. Prioritize: (1) trust signals like "no fee unless you win", (2) urgency around the adjuster/insurance angle, (3) making the call feel easy and safe, (4) accident-specific content that makes visitors feel seen and understood.
